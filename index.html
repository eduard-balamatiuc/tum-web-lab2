Our Story

Founded in 2015, Bloom & Petal began with a simple mission: to create stunning floral arrangements that bring joy and beauty to everyday life.

Our dedicated team of floral designers combines traditional techniques with modern aesthetics to craft unique arrangements for any occasion.

We source our flowers from local sustainable farms whenever possible, ensuring the freshest blooms while supporting our community.
